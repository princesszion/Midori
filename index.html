About Us

Midori Agro Ventures ltd is an agro marketing and production company that provides sustainable agricultural market solutions to small and medium scale farmers

by giving them access to sustainable agricultural markets and promoting value addition for small and medium scale farmers in Zambia and beyond.

Our Vision

Becoming the leading provider of sustainable agricultural market solutions for small and medium scale farmers in Zambia and beyond.

Our Mission

Providing sustainable agricultural market solutions by facilitating market linkages between farmers
and off takers and promoting value addition for small and medium scale farmers in Zambia and beyond.

Our Services

Create Market Links

Providing market linkages between farmers and offtakers

Value Addition

Promoting value addition among farmers

Stabilizing Supply

Ensuring a steady and stable supply of agricultural goods to offtakers.

Increase Production

Running production at various Midori farms

Agribusiness Training and Capacity Building

Offer training and capacity-building services,empowering small and medium scale farmers.

Agricultural Technology Consultation

Consultation services to help farmers adopt and integrate modern agricultural technologies into their operations.
